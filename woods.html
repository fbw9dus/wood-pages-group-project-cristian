  • Balsam fir(Abies balsamea)
  • Larch(Larix)
  • White pine
  • White cedar
  • Nootka cypress(Cupressus nootkatensis)

Softwood is sometimes used as a substitute for hardwood, primarily because it's
generally cheaper. But there are other reasons, including the fact that it's usually lighter
and easier to cut and shape as it yields more readily to cutting tools and machines.
